History

Chelsea Football Club is an English football club which was founded in 10 March 1905 by Gus Mears. In early years, Chelsea FC yo-yoed between the First and Second Divisions.

In 2003, the club is owned by a Russian business man, Roman Abramovich, and under his supports Chelsea F.C. won the first place of Premier League 3 times, Cup 8 times, and UEFA Chamipons League once.
